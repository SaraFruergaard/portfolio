About Bright Green Summit

The only trade association in Sweden dedicated to U.S.-Swedish trade and investment. Since 1992, we have promoted U.S.-Swedish trade and investment and U.S. commercial interests in Sweden.

  • Target Audience
    The project is intended for companies, policymakers in Sweden and the U.S., students, and attendees of The Bright Green Summit.
  • Problem Statement
    The Bright Green Summit aims to revolutionize public engagement and comprehension in the critical areas of climate change and sustainability. The complexity and often abstract nature of environmental issues create a barrier to broader public understanding and involvement. Moreover, there's a need to illuminate the synergistic efforts between academia, business, and government in tackling these global challenges.
  • Project Outcome
    The primary goal is to transform the Bright Green Summit into an immersive, technology-driven experience that goes beyond traditional conference formats. Success involves using innovative technology to deepen engagement with environmental issues and attract a diverse audience.
